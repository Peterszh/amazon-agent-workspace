# Inventory Risk Checker

Use this skill when inventory and sales trend data are available.

## Check
- stock coverage days
- inbound timing
- out-of-stock risk
- slow-moving inventory
- replenishment urgency

## Output
Return:
1. inventory risk level
2. stockout risk
3. slow-moving risk
4. suggested replenishment action
