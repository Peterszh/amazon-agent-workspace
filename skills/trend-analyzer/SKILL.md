# Trend Analyzer

Use this skill to compare time periods.

- 14d vs previous 14d
- 30d vs previous 30d

## Focus
- sales trend
- traffic trend
- conversion trend
- spend trend
- profitability trend

## Output
Return:
1. trend summary
2. strongest movement
3. most likely concern area
