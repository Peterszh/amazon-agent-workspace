# Ad Search Term Analyzer

Use this skill on ad search term reports.

## Focus metrics
- impressions
- clicks
- ctr
- cpc
- orders
- conversion rate
- spend
- sales
- acos
- roas

## Group terms into:
- high_click_high_conversion
- high_click_low_conversion
- low_click_high_conversion
- costly_non_converting
- irrelevant_or_low_match

## Output
Return:
1. harvest keywords
2. negative keyword candidates
3. title-worthy terms
4. bullet-worthy terms
5. advertising notes
