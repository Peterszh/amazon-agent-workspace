# Ad Performance Analyzer

Use this skill when ad metrics are available.

## Analyze
- impressions
- clicks
- ctr
- cpc
- conversion rate
- spend
- acos
- tacos
- roas
- ad order share

## Diagnose patterns
- high spend low conversion
- high ctr low cvr
- low ctr strong cvr
- branded vs generic imbalance
- budget inefficiency

## Output
Return:
1. ad diagnosis
2. likely causes
3. 7-day ad actions
4. 30-day optimization ideas
